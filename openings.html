Current Openings

Graduate Students

Students interested in joining the UC Davis Computational Linguistics Lab should apply directly to the UC Davis Linguistics Graduate Program or the UC Davis Computer Science Program.

Undergraduate Students

There are occasional openings for undergraduate research. Current UC Davis undergraduates interested in joining the UC Davis Computational Linguistics Lab should contact one of the lab directors. With few exceptions, students should first take LIN 127 and/or LIN 177.
