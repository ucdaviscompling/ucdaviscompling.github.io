The 15th International Conference on Parsing Technologies

Pisa, September 20-22, 2017


IWPT 2017 continues the tradition of biennial meetings on parsing technology organized by SIGPARSE, the Special Interest Group on Natural Language Parsing of the Association for Computational Linguistics (ACL).

This year IWPT will take place in Pisa, Italy, within easy reach of most of Europe through its international airport.


Important dates

Submission deadline Wednesday, July 5, 2017
Notification of acceptance Wednesday, August 9, 2017
Final papers due Wednesday, August 30, 2017
Conference Wednesday to Friday, September 20-22, 2017
